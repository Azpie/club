Special day trips & holidays

Below you will find details of our special walking day trips and holiday's, all of which are dog friendly!

All trips and holiday's include transportation for you and your canine and accommodation where applicable*

Suffolk Coast

Saturday 14th July - Price £50

Leaving time: 7.00am

Return time(approx):7.00pm

Meeting point: Memorial Center

A beautiful day trip to the suffolk coast, you and your dog will get to enjoy the sea air and a wonderful walk along the beach.

We will then stop for lunch at Dogfish Cove a dog friendly, pirate-themed restaurant.

Next we will split into 2 groups;

Group 1; A long walk along the promenande (approximately 3 miles)
Group 2; A leisurely walk along the pier, frequent stops to sit and enjoy the view.

Be sure to dress for the weather and bring a towel to dry off!

Lake District

Friday 16th October to Monday 19th October - Price £400

Leaving time: 9.00am

Return time(approx):6.00pm

Meeting point:Village Hall

A long weekend adventure to the breathtaking lake district.

You will be staying in a beautiful log cabin with your canine companion with all mod cons, including a hot tub (for human use only).

There are a selection of cabins available that can accommadate from 2 adults all the way up to 3 families (average family of 2 adults/2 children) either self-catering or half board.

We have a variety of activites and walks planned around this stunning retreat, catering to young and old.

Please get in touch for full details!

*Prices are for 1 adult and 1 dog, please get in touch for details of family rates
